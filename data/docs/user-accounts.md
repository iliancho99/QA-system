# User Accounts

This page describes how user accounts work, including how to create one and how
to manage your sign-in details.

## Creating an account

To create an account, provide an email address and choose a password. A
confirmation message is sent to the address you supply. Accounts remain inactive
until the confirmation link is followed.

## Resetting your password

If you forget your password, use the reset option on the sign-in screen. Enter
your email address and follow the link that is sent to you to choose a new
password.

## Deleting an account

You may delete your account at any time from the account settings page. Deletion
is permanent and removes all data associated with the account.
